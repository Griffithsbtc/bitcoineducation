Essential Reading

The books that shaped how the Bitcoin community thinks. Start with The Bitcoin Standard, then go wherever your curiosity takes you.

The Bitcoin Standard
Saifedean Ammous
A foundational book on Bitcoin, exploring the history of money, the flaws of fiat currency, and why Bitcoin is the best candidate for sound money in the digital age.
The Fiat Standard
Saifedean Ammous
A follow-up to The Bitcoin Standard, analysing the global fiat monetary system, its problems, and how Bitcoin offers a viable alternative.
Principles of Economics
Saifedean Ammous
A classic economics textbook foundational to Austrian economics, which underpins much of Bitcoin's economic philosophy.
Broken Money
Lyn Alden
Explores the history of money, the problems with current monetary systems, and how Bitcoin and other innovations could fix broken money.
Mastering Bitcoin (3rd Ed)
Antonopoulos & Harding
The technical bible. How Bitcoin works at every level. Open-source and essential for developers.
Mastering the Lightning Network
Antonopoulos, Osuntokun & Pickhardt
The definitive guide to Bitcoin's Layer 2. How instant, cheap Bitcoin payments work.
The Internet of Money (Vol 1)
Andreas Antonopoulos
A collection of talks by one of Bitcoin's best educators, explaining why Bitcoin matters beyond price, and how it represents a new paradigm for money and society.
The Internet of Money (Vol 2)
Andreas Antonopoulos
Continues with more talks and essays, delving deeper into the philosophical, social, and economic implications of Bitcoin and open blockchain technology.
Check Your Financial Privilege
Alex Gladstein
Examines how Bitcoin is used as a tool for financial freedom in countries with unstable currencies or authoritarian regimes, highlighting stories from around the world.
The Sovereign Individual
Davidson & Rees-Mogg
A prescient book from 1997 predicting the rise of digital money and the decline of nation-state power. Explores how technology will empower individuals and disrupt traditional institutions.
Gradually, Then Suddenly
Parker A. Lewis
A collection of essays breaking down complex Bitcoin topics into easy-to-understand concepts, focusing on how Bitcoin adoption happens slowly at first, then all at once.
The Little Bitcoin Book
Bitcoin Collective
A concise, accessible introduction to Bitcoin by a group of international authors. Explains what Bitcoin is, why it matters, and how it can empower people globally.
Bitcoin is Venice
Allen Farrington & Sacha Meyers
Essays on the past and future of capitalism through the lens of Bitcoin and sound money.
Resistance Money
Bailey, Rettler & Warmke
Explores how Bitcoin can be used as a tool for resistance against censorship, authoritarianism, and financial repression.
The Basics of Bitcoins and Blockchains
Antony Lewis
A clear, jargon-free introduction to cryptocurrencies and the technology that powers them.
21
21 Lessons
Gigi
What falling down the Bitcoin rabbit hole taught one person about philosophy, economics, and technology. Free to read online.
