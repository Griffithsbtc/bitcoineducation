Performance & Value

Bitcoin has been the best-performing asset class of the last decade. Not stocks. Not property. Not gold. Here's the data, the context, and the people making the case.

The Crash Narrative

Every four years, like clockwork, Bitcoin goes through a cycle. The media declares it dead. Then it breaks all-time highs. Understanding this pattern is the difference between panic selling and conviction holding. This isn't a Trump rally or a meme coin pump. It's a 15-year monetary revolution playing out in predictable waves.
